Project 1: Projectile Motion

This model shows the projectile motion of an object that clears a barrier, passes through a hoop, and reaches a target. You can access the video, showing the projectile motion of the object, by clicking on the title above for this project.



Project 2: Collision Between Two Objects in Motion

This model shows two objects in motion that eventually collide with each other; one is in projectile motion while the other is moving down an inclined plane. You can access the link to the video by clicking on the title of the project above.



Project 3: Snell's Law

This model demonstrates Snell's Law. Light enters from the laser box source and refracts when it reaches the water in the container. The light changes direction at that point, which is shown by the pencil markings leaving the other side of the box.
